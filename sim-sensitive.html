Problem

Old website has outdated design and problems with usability. When users open the main page, it is difficult for them to get an idea what products company has in general and what they are for. Information about the new arrivals was posted just in Instagram and the website does not have a straight link to the new products. Payment process is made using external website and it is not immediately clear how to buy something.

Solution

Company actively uses social media to advertise its producs. But people use website for buying so it has to be a pleasant experience. Therefore I improved the architecture and payment process, made structured catalogs and clear search.

Style

Every design decision should have a reason. Main colors are black and white with additional palette of bright accents to emphasize brand’s style. I added some colorful animations and pictures of colored hair to make products more attractive to clients.
